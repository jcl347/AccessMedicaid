I don't know which plan I have

Here are easy ways to find out:

  • Look at your Medi-Cal member ID card — the plan name and Member Services phone number are printed on it.
  • Sign in or check at BenefitsCal.com.
  • Call Health Care Options at 1‑800‑430‑4263 (free) to ask which plan you're in or to change plans.

🗣️ Help in your language

Every Medi-Cal plan must give you a free interpreter — by phone or in person — in any language, including American Sign Language. You never have to bring your own, and you should never have to rely on a child to translate.

How to ask: When you call any number on this page, say the name of your language (for example, "Español," "中文," "Tiếng Việt," "한국어," "Հայերեն," "Tagalog"). They will connect an interpreter.

You can also read this page in another language using your browser's built‑in Translate feature (right‑click → Translate, or the translate icon in your address bar).

About this guide

Access Medi-Cal LA is a free navigation guide for Medi-Cal (Medicaid) members in Los Angeles County. It gathers the phone numbers and links you need into one calm, simple place.

⚠️ Please double-check before you rely on a number. This guide is not the government and is not your health plan. Plans, phone numbers, and benefits can change. Always confirm important details with your plan's Member Services line or your county office. In a medical emergency, call 911.
